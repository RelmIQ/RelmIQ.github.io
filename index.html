What We Audit

Six Areas We Examine
In Every Domo Audit

In 30 minutes, we cover the areas that have the biggest impact on your Domo ROI, team efficiency, and data reliability.

⚡

Credit Consumption

We identify where your Domo credits are going — duplicate dataflows, uncached cards, unnecessary refreshes — and show you exactly how to cut waste.

🔗

Connector Health

Review your data connectors for failures, stale pipelines, and missing error handling. Broken connectors mean decisions made on bad data.

🧮

Dataflow Architecture

Analyse your ETL and Magic ETL structure for inefficiencies, redundancy, and opportunities to simplify — including Beast Mode logic.

📊

Dashboard Governance

Assess your card and dashboard organisation, ownership hygiene, and whether your dashboards are actually being used by the right people.

🔐

Security & Permissions

Check user roles, PDP policies, and data access controls to ensure sensitive data is protected and access is appropriately scoped.

Common Findings

What We Typically Find

Based on audits of Domo environments across industries, these are the most common issues we uncover.

✓

Duplicate dataflows running the same transformation multiple times

✓

Connectors failing silently with no alerts configured

✓

Beast Mode calculations that should live in dataflows

✓

Dashboards no one has opened in over 90 days consuming credits

✓

Users with admin access who should have restricted roles

✓

Snowflake or cloud warehouse sync issues causing data lag

✓

No PDP (Personalised Data Permissions) policies in place

✓

Manual data uploads replacing automatable connectors
